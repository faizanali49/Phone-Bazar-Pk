# PhoneBazar PK

PhoneBazar PK is a modern marketplace platform designed for buying and selling both new and certified used smartphones in Pakistan. The platform focuses on popular brands such as Apple (iPhone), Samsung, Vivo, Oppo, and Xiaomi Redmi, offering customers a trusted and transparent shopping experience.

The project addresses common challenges faced by Pakistani buyers, including uncertainty about device condition, PTA approval status, battery health, warranty coverage, and authenticity. Through detailed device inspections, condition grading, and trust-focused product listings, users can confidently compare and purchase smartphones online.

The platform also enables customers to trade in old devices, explore certified used phones, browse new arrivals, and purchase accessories such as chargers, earbuds, power banks, and phone cases. The design follows a clean, premium, and mobile-first approach inspired by modern tech marketplaces.
